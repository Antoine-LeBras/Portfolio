Séquence,
a cinema app

Where

Nantes, France

Typologie

Fictive project

Duration

2 months

Field

UX-UI design

Context & objective

A fictitious project with the aim of creating an application that would support a milieu, a project. This is how "Séquence" was born, an application enabling anyone to support the world of independent cinema (support for film financing, cinemas supporting independent films, genre films...). The latter are often associative cinemas, centered on the community, sharing a passion for cinema.

This is how, after gathering a lot of information from cinema fans and ideation workshops, we came up with this application. It includes a film library where the community can rate and classify films. To finance films for free, you can use your points to support film projects, cinemas... To collect points, all you have to do is link your streaming platform accounts, and the more you watch, the more points you earn. Also, before various content, users can watch an ad to collect new points.
