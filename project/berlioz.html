Berlioz project

Where

Paris, France

Work with

PM, CSM and developers

Duration

3 months

Field

UX design, product design

Context

The aim of this application is to make it easier for company employees to access their assigned missions. Users can consult their assignments, choose whether or not to accept them, and access a detailed history as well as all the information they need to make an informed decision. In addition, users can indicate their availability, enabling them to receive assignment proposals from their company.

Objective

Redesign of the mobile application for company employees. The application had numerous bugs in displaying information or logging in, and was no longer updated on a regular basis.

Work

The application becoming a webapp, it was necessary to rework all the connection paths as well as the first reconnection of users on the platform. Also the tree structure was reworked in its entirety to help the user as much as possible in his use of the webapp.

In addition to the various flows, a visual overhaul was carried out. It had to respect maximum accessibility criteria (WCAG) through a neutral, modern design.

Finally, work was carried out on the information hierarchy, which was completely overhauled. This was achieved via a number of card-sorting workshops, in order to understand what information was necessary and most important to receive in different places (Mail, SMS, detail page...).
