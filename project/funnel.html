Candidate qualification funnel

Where

Paris, France

Work with

PM, CSM and developers

Duration

2 months

Field

UX design, product design

Context & Objective

Establish a qualification process for candidates. The objective was to speed up the qualification process of candidates by retrieving a lot of information (documents, etc.) and to make them quickly available on the same platform for recruiters and managers.

This opportunity represented a qualified registration module that had been lacking until then. There was no easy way to sign up for the app and allow users to more easily enter a qualification process before accessing and appearing at managers to receive assignments.

Work

To fully understand these needs, we interviewed several hiring managers and managers who reviewed the profiles before validating them. We have identified their needs in terms of information on the profiles to qualify in order to engage them on proposed missions, especially since we operate in the health sector, where certain crucial information is necessary to carry out missions.

It was essential to organize the process based on a benchmark of studies, methods and laws. We have designed a registration path that meets the needs of users, managers and recruitment units, ensuring that we obtain the minimum necessary information while maximizing qualification, without compromising or frustrating the employee’s experience.

To do this, we created several journey flows and tested them with end users to assess their adequacy with the expectations in terms of potential information collected, while taking into account the perspective of possible employee frustration. We thought about how far to go in this journey, while examining which features could be highlighted, including the importance of validating the profile before receiving missions and the incentive to add availability for future missions.

This project was then tested with new end users, involving not only employees trying the course, but also users managers and HR managers.
